Baked Tofu

Recipe Link

Why This Recipe

This is bussin tofu that actually tastes good, you should use firm tofu for best results. Take a break from meat and try this you will enjoy it.

Ingredients

  • 14 ounces firm tofu, patted dry and cube
  • 1/2 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablesoons tamari
  • 1/2 teaspoon sriracha

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425Â°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Toss the cubed tofu with the olive oil, tamari, and sriracha. For extra crispy tofu, sprinkle with the cornstarch and gently toss to coat.
  3. Spread the tofu evenly onto the baking sheet. Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until browned around the edges. Remove and serve warm.
